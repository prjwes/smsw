==============================================
SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM - QUICK START GUIDE
==============================================

STEP 1: INSTALL DATABASE
------------------------
1. Open browser: http://localhost/phpmyadmin
2. Create database: "school_management"
3. Click Import tab
4. Choose file: database/schema.sql
5. Click GO button
6. Wait for success message

STEP 2: LOGIN
-------------
1. Open browser: http://localhost/school-management-system/
2. Username: admin
3. Password: admin123
4. Click Login

STEP 3: CHANGE PASSWORD
-----------------------
1. Click your name (top right)
2. Click Settings
3. Scroll to "Change Password"
4. Enter current: admin123
5. Enter new password (2x)
6. Click Change Password

STEP 4: ADD TEACHERS (For Timetable)
-------------------------------------
1. Click "Timetable" in sidebar
2. Scroll to "Add Teacher & Assign Subjects"
3. Enter teacher name
4. Check subject-grade boxes
5. Click "Add Teacher"
6. Repeat for all teachers

STEP 5: GENERATE TIMETABLE
---------------------------
1. Still in Timetable page
2. Scroll to "Generate Timetable"
3. Name: "Term 1 2025"
4. Type: Secondary
5. Check: Grade 7, 8, 9
6. Click "Generate Timetable"
7. Click "View" to see it

STEP 6: ADD STUDENTS
--------------------
1. Click "Students" in sidebar
2. Click "Add Student"
3. Fill in details
4. Click "Add Student"
5. Save the generated password

THAT'S IT! System is ready to use.
